LEVEL CHART — HOW IT WORKS

The Level Chart is a recovery betting calculator based on the martingale method.

Enter your starting balance, bet size, and multiplier. The chart shows you exactly how much to bet at each level to recover all previous losses with one win.

For example: Start with ₹10 → if you lose, bet ₹20 → lose again, bet ₹40 → win at level 3 → profit covers all losses.

The ✅ Safe indicator means your balance is enough to cover that level. The ⚠ means you would run out of balance — do not go beyond that level.

💡 Use 1.5x multiplier for safer play. Use 2x for standard Wingo payout recovery.
